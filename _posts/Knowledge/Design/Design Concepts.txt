
Basic principles or Pillars of OOP(Object oriented Programming)

There are four principles/concept of OOP:
1. Abstraction
2. Polymorphism
3. Inheritance 
4. Encapsulation

The above can be remembered with word A-PIE.

Abstraction

Abstraction is about emphasis on idea ignoring the details.  The basis idea of the abstraction is to simply the complexity of a system.
